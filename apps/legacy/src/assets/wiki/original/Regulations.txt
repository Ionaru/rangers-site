* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Class 1 Sight '''</span> - Anyone can choose his Class 1 sight.
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Element Sight Upgrade'''</span> - Each Element (Squad, Platoon Element) can upgrade one soldier with a Class 2 sights.
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Sight Upgrade Restriction'''</span> - Following roles are restricted to Class 1 sights and cannot upgrade:
** Platoon Leader
** Platoon Medic
** Squad Leader
** Combat Medic
** Autorifleman
** Breacher
** Battlerifle
** Grenadier
** Medium Machine Gunner
** Medium AT Gunner
** Heavy AT Gunner
** Mortar Gunner
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Platoon Element Sights'''</span> - The Platoon Sergeant and one additional Member can upgrade to a Class 2 Sight. A Platoon DMR counts as a upgrade.

== AT-Weapons ==
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''AT Limit'''</span> - Each Squad can carry up to three AT-Weapons.
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''MAT/HAT Multiplier'''</span> - The presence of a MAT/HAT in an element counts as two AT-Weapons.
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Restriction'''</span> - Following roles cannot carry AT-Weapons:
** Autorifleman
** Medium Machine Gunner
** MMG Assistant
** MAT Assistant

== Additional Ammunition ==
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Additional Ammunition'''</span> - Each soldier can carry additional ammunition for the AR, MMG and MAT after permission by his leader:
** 200 Rnds of Machine Gun Ammunition (AR or MMG)
** 1x MAT Rocket
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Restriction'''</span> - No soldier who has the abovementioned ammunition in his loadout is allowed to carry additional ammunition.

== Special Gear ==
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Lockpicks'''</span> - One member in the Squad can carry Lockpicks (preferably the Medic).
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Entrenching Tool'''</span> - Each Element can carry two Entrenching Tools (Leader and Medic are not permitted to carry an Entrenching Tool).
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Laserdesignator'''</span> - The Platoon Element may carry one Laserdesignator.
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''AN/PRC-152'''</span> - Each Fire Team Leader may carry an AN/PRC-152 (at least the 2nd in Command has to, 3rd in Command may carry one turned off until needed).
* <span style='color:#087b08'>'''Special Mission Gear'''</span> - Special Mission Gear (e.g. Laptop) is being distributed by the Platoon Leader, the restrictions from the additional ammunition section apply.
